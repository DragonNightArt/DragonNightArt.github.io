Request a Commission

How do I request a commission?

Send an Email. Please include these details with your request:
What you intend to do with the work. (This helps me know what size to work at.) What style you want.
As many details as possible about the character(s) you want to be drawn. If the artwork is of someone or something specific, please include a reference image.
If you want the image to be worked on my Twitch stream, if you want it to stay private, or if you have no preference either way.

Thumbnails and In-Progress Previews

Shortly after receiving a request, I will return the email with a thumbnail and price estimate.
A thumbnail is a small, quick sketch. It is meant to work out the shapes and general feel of the piece.
When you have confirmed the thumbnail design, I will start work proper.

Price

I charge $10 + ($10 per hour) USD for my work. I count time per half-hour.
The final price sometimes varies from the estimate by $5 or $10. The final price will not exceed the estimate by more than 30%.
For Commissions over $30, I'll request up to 50% in advance.

Time

Images are normally completed within 2 weeks.

Final

As soon as payment has been received I'll send you all the project files, including the full resolution .png image.
